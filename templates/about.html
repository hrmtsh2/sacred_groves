About Our Project & Team

Our Mission: Mapping and Preserving India's Sacred Groves

We are a team of passionate individuals – Saurav, Abhijeet, Arnish, and Aayush – dedicated to documenting the invaluable sacred groves of India. Guided by our mentor, Aasani sir, our project aims to create a comprehensive digital repository and interactive map of these unique biocultural heritage sites.

Sacred groves are vital ecosystems, rich in biodiversity and deeply intertwined with local traditions and beliefs. Our goal is to raise awareness about their significance, support conservation efforts, and provide a valuable resource for researchers, students, and the general public. We believe that by mapping these treasures, we can contribute to their long-term protection and celebrate India's rich natural and cultural legacy.
